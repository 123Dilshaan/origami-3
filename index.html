Sakana: Japanese word for "Fish"



  • The fish is a symbol for happiness, well being and freedom.

  • It also symbolises strength, determination and courage which are needed to swim upstream.



Tsuru: Japanese word for "Crane"



  • Crane symbolises long life according to Japanese folklore.

  • It is also a symbol of happiness, peace and good luck.
